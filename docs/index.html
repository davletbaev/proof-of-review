The Problem

Our team consists of members who have been around the cryptocurrency sector since 2013 and seen the same cycles for many projects.

The Problem

We have seen several cycles of growth in this emerging industry, but there is an underlying issue remains for investors into the space. How do they analyse projects from the thousands out there and how to identify projects with potential while avoiding so-called "scam" projects.

The metrics used in this sector can often be different from those used to analyse projects in other markets, and most market participants don't have to experience or knowledge to identify these.

As a result, the target market for projects is left uninformed, and this crowd of investors often relies on price moments to raise their awareness in a project, rather than the fundamentals of a project.

The Solution

At Proof of Review, we aim to solve these problems by providing institutional grade reviews on projects including POW coins, Tokens, Exchanges, Mining Pools, Security Token Offerings and Initial Exchange Offerings.

The Solution

We utilise our experience, knowledge and expertise to review these projects and share these reports to the public via our website and our social media following.

The review aims to provide a detailed analysis for a project which is unbiased and covers all the necessary elements for an investor to be informed.
